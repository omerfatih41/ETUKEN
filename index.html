About the Team

**etuken Aviation Team** emerges from the vibrant technological ecosystem of **Erzurum Technical University Technology Teams**. We are driven by a singular mission: to master the design and execution of sophisticated Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS). Our dedication to innovation has been proven on the national stage, having participated in the prestigious TEKNOFEST competition for two consecutive years, culminating in an impressive **9th place finish at TEKNOFEST 2024**. We transform challenging technical specifications into flight-ready, highly autonomous solutions, pushing the boundaries of aerial robotics and system integration.
